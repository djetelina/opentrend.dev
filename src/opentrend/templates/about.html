about

This project was created because I got bored of browsing through my GitHub insights and refreshing stars. I wanted to know how my projects were catching on. If they're being useful, or just something I shared for the sake of sharing.

Your OAuth tokens are stored encrypted on my own hardware. They are very sensitive and they're treated as such.

All package statistics are considered your own by default, the only stat we expose is the custom reach score and the fact you are using opentrend. Everything else, despite most of it being public, is considered yours.

Feature requests and issue reports are welcome on GitHub. If I don't forget, it should also have a sponsorship thingy, to cover hosting costs (especially if this grows beyond my small servers).
